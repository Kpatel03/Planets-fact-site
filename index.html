EARTH

Third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to harbor life. About 29.2% of Earth's surface is land with remaining 70.8% covered with water. Earth's distance from the Sun, physical properties and geological history have allowed life to evolve and thrive.

Source: Wikipedia

Earth has a layered internal structure composed of a crust, mantle, outer core, and inner core. The inner core is solid, while the outer core is liquid.

Earth's surface geology includes mountains, volcanoes, oceans, and tectonic plates. Erosion, weathering, and sedimentation shape the surface.
